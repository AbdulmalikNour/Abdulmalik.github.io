Projects

Pong or smth

Pong or smth is a fresh take on the classic Pong formula, adding a dynamic twist with random power-ups! As players rally back and forth, they fill up a meter that, when full, grants one of five random abilities. These abilities can double damage, invert the opponent’s controls, strengthen shots, and more! The goal? Knock your opponent’s health down to zero in intense, fast-paced local multiplayer matches.

Hitless

Hitless is a top-down 2D shooter inspired by Hotline Miami and John Wick. It blends fast-paced gunplay with creative improvisation—if you're out of ammo, anything within reach becomes a weapon. Throw objects, shoot, and kick your way through relentless enemies. When your combo meter starts shaking and turning red, it's your cue to unleash a brutal finishing move—eliminating an enemy in style while restoring your health, because in this game, you'll be dodging bullets just as much as you're firing them!

Untitled Project

Untitled Project is a precision platformer focused on aerial maneuverability, where you use your abilities to ascend from the depths of a pit back to the surface. Though still in the prototype stage, this project represents a culmination of lessons learned from past games. With a strong focus on scope and refined mechanics, this is the game I’m fully committed to bringing to completion and release.
